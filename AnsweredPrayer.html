Answered Prayer

Guy is on his knees praying.

“Lord. I don’t know why you’ve sent this down but I-”

*click*

GOD picks up a flip phone made out of praying hands

“Yeeelllow?”

“God?! Is that you?”

“You called me, why are you surprised? You didn’t believe in me? Ha! I’m just pulling your rib, buddy! What’s up?”

“Well, uh, Lord. We are battling a horrible disease and everyone is suffering and I was wondering if you could maybe bring us peace.”

“Ok let’s take a look here, what planet are you calling from again?”

“Uh...Earth?”

“Earth huh? Hang on a minute”

GOD looks through maps and charts “Earth…...earth…….earth….AH! Here it is. HEY! That’s where I was keeping my dinosaurs, what are you guys doing there? What happened to my--
Ah, whatever. So you say there’s a plague on Earth?”

“Yes Lord, and I was hoping you could help us”

“Okay, see plagues are actually Satan’s department. Want me to transfer you?”

“We just need some help here, if you could maybe get rid of the plague or something, that’d be awesome.”

*sigh* “If it wasn’t for him this job would be SO much better. Okay, just a couple of housekeeping questions here - have you guys been singing my praises?”

“Yes Lord! Every Sunday we sing our hearts out for you!”

“Good, good. Okay, does everyone wear a bottle around their neck filled with the blood of the innocent?”

“No...what? No, we don’t do that.”

“That was a trick question, just making sure you weren’t one of Satan’s guys wasting my time again. Okay, one more: Nobody eats for 2 days after a full moon, right?”

“Is that another trick question?”

“No, I’m totally serious. That’s the most important rule I ever made are you SERIOUS?”

“I’ve never heard of that rule before!”

“Ok what version of the Bible are you guys using?”

“Version? Um, I’m not sure what you mean, uh, the King James version?”

“Who? No! I mean what version? If you’re not using the updated one, that’s probably what the problem is. What happens to Jesus in the one you read?”

“He gets crucified and then comes back to life.”

“Oh my me, are you kidding right now? You guys are actually using- look, that's not even an official version. One of the Angels wrote that one as a joke when we did the Roast of Jesus. No way man, you guys are way off. How did you even get that?!”

“Well, to be honest I’m not sure. It was kind of passed down from generation to generation. Maybe something about Dead Sea Scrolls? I’m not sure.”

“You based your entire life on a book and you don’t even know where it CAME from?! Oh wow. Now I really miss the dinosaurs. I really should do more dinosaur planets. Okay look, I’m gonna send a messenger down there with the latest Bible. Call me back in about 3 or 4 hundred years, and if everybody follows the rules, I’ll help you out, okay? Thanks for calling, have a blessed day!”

*God hangs up*
